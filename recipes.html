Spaghetti with tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Water
  • Sugar

Procedure

Prepping the Sauce

If you have freshly prepared tomatoes:

  1. Slice around 3 cloves of garlic longways
  2. Put a couple of tablespoons of olive oil in a pot, enough to barely cover the bottom and putn on low medium heat
  3. Once oil has warmed up a bit, place sliced garlic in the oil
  4. Wait for the garlic to get a little darker, but be careful not to burn the garlic, or it will skunk the sauce
  5. Place uncooked sauce in the pot
  6. Add a few tablespoons of sugar, and a teaspoon of salt
  7. Let sauce cook for about 20 minutes, stiring frequently

If you have a premade jar of sauce, then you're ready:

Pasta

  1. If you have a premade jar of sauce, then you're ready
  2. Fill up a pot more than halfway with cold water
  3. Let water boil
  4. Place Spaghetti in boiling water
  5. Immediately add a few tablespoons of salt
  6. Stirring frequently, let pasta boil for about 7-8 minutes
  7. When happy with the texture of the pasta, strain the water
  8. Add sauce to pasta
  9. Enjoy

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Cheese of choice
  • Sliced Bread
  • Butter
  1. Butter the Bread
  2. Place bread butter-side-down on the frying pan
  3. Place your choice of cheese between the bread. Mix different types of cheese, double up, Get creative!
  4. Let the bread get colored, the darker the better (not black)
  5. Flip the sandwich and let the other side cook
  6. Enjoy

Italian Sausage and Peppers

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Bread
  • Olive Oil
  • Your choice of bell-peppers
  • Sweet Onion
  1. Slice a quarter onion longways
  2. Slice your choice of peppers longways
  3. Place a couple table spoons of olive oil into a frying pan
  4. Once the oil heats up a bit, place the sliced onions and peppers
  5. Wait for colorization of onions and for softening of peppers
  6. Place sausage on a barbeque, and wait for colorization of sausage
  7. Once sausage is cooked, place it within a bun, and placing a generous amount of peppers and onions in as well
  8. Enjoy
